What is PRISMTECH?

PRISMTECH is a 24-hour IEEE hackathon for practical innovation.

Teams prototype solutions across AI, cybersecurity, sustainability, healthcare, smart infrastructure, and human-centered education with guidance from mentors, faculty, and industry experts.

About the Hackathon

Objective, format, eligibility, and participant benefits.

The objective is to transform student ideas into credible prototypes with social, technical, and entrepreneurial value. Participants may choose from domains including AI/ML, cybersecurity, IoT, fintech, sustainability, healthcare, accessibility, education technology, and smart cities.

  • Open to undergraduate and postgraduate students from recognized institutions.
  • Teams of 2-4 members; one participant acts as team leader.
  • Hybrid-ready 24-hour build sprint with mentoring, evaluation, final pitch, and demo.
  • Benefits include portfolio projects, IEEE exposure, leadership opportunities, and industry interaction.

    Rules and Guidelines

    Clear rules for fair innovation

    • Eligible students must carry institutional ID and register once only.
    • Submissions must include project summary, presentation, GitHub link, and demo link.
    • Judging criteria: innovation, feasibility, impact, technical depth, UX, and pitch quality.
    • Code of conduct, plagiarism, safety violations, or disruptive behavior can lead to disqualification.
    • Teams retain intellectual property; shared sponsor datasets must follow stated license terms.

    FAQ

    Common participant questions

    Is there a registration fee?

    Fee details can be configured by admins. Scholarships or waivers may be announced.

    Can I form a team after registering?

    Yes, team details can be edited before the registration deadline.

    Are food and accommodation provided?

    Meals, breaks, first-aid, and sick-room support are planned. Accommodation depends on availability.

    What should participants bring?

    Laptop, charger, ID card, required software, and backup internet access.

    Who is eligible?

    Students from recognized colleges and universities with valid institutional ID.

    What is the refund policy?

    Refund or cancellation terms will be published with the official rulebook.
